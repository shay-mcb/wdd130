Home Page

Welcome to Yogi Writes, a portfolio showcasing all of Shay McBride's written works. Here you'll find updates to current projects, finished short stories that you can read in full, and links to completed novels. There may also be a hint or too about writing mindfully ;) Thanks for being here!

Images for the Home page

About Me Page

Hey there! I'm Shay, a yogi who writes. I'm a passionate yoga teacher and story teller that finds the best form of meditation to be with a notebook in one hand and a pen in the other. Some of my works include Talentless and Joy of the Rain. Glad you could be here! If you have any questions, feel free to contact me and follow me on my YouTube channel, Yogi_Writes.
